About the Basketball Africa League

Our Mission

The Basketball Africa League (BAL) is committed to advancing and elevating the sport of basketball across the African continent. By providing a competitive arena for elite athletes, the BAL highlights Africa's exceptional talent and promotes the growth of the sport at all levels. In partnership with the NBA, we strive to inspire a passion for basketball, engage fans from diverse communities, and build a vibrant community that celebrates the game. Through this collaboration, the BAL aspires to create lasting opportunities for players, coaches, and fans alike, fueling a deeper connection to basketball throughout Africa.

Our History

Founded in 2020, the Basketball Africa League (BAL) represents a significant milestone in the world of sports, created through a historic partnership between the NBA and FIBA.
